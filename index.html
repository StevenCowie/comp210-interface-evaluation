Kick your astronaut is a Squirrelsoft Studios game designed in Unity.You play as an astronaut and take part in variety of fun mini games featuring different objectives such as deathmatch, space hockey, grab the belt and dodge the crate. It is set in a spaceship and is desgined for the casual gamer. The game can be played by yourself or with friends to make it even more fun!

This website is designed as an interface evaluation report on this game.
